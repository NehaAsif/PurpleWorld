What is Purple World?


Purple world is a world of BTS.BTS, also known as the Bangtan Boys, is a seven-member South Korean boy band that began formation in 2010 and debuted in 2013 under Big Hit Entertainment(known as HYBE these days) .
Once you enter their world their is no going back.Their hardwork started to inspire you,their lyrics,opinions,little habbits make you fall in love with them.The BTS members each struggled in their own way to make it to where they are today, despite all of the odds against them.
Each member has their own individual personality and talents that help make BTS so amazing!....But remeber their is no exist door in this world :)
